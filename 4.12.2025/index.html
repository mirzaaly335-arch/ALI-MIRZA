Characteristics Of Musical Sound

Intensity

Power of a wave per unit perpendicular area is called its Timberintensity.It is denaoted by "I".Its SI unit is W/m2.

Loudness

The magnitude of auditory sensation produced by a sound is called its loudness.It is characteristics due to which we can identify a laud and feint sound.

Pitch

The characteristics due to which we can identify a shrill and grave sound is called its "pitch".High frequency sound have a high pitch and low frequncy sound have a low pitch.

Quality

The characteristic of sound due to which we can identify the source of sound is called its Quality.
