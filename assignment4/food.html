Fast food

Burger

  • Soft bun with meat or veggie patty.
  • Filled with sauce, cheese, and salad.
  • Easy to eat and very tasty.

Sandwich

  • Two bread slices with filling inside.
  • Light and quick to make.
  • Good for breakfast or lunch.

Pizza

  • Flat bread with cheese on top.
  • Many toppings like chicken or vegetables.
  • Baked and shared with friends.
